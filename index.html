Title: Deaf-blind communication using Arduino

Description: Communication is key in humans. But communication between two people who are Deaf and blind is hindered. Deaf-blind communication has come a lot way from using sign language, Face-to-Face Communication System such as Screen Braille Communicator. The blind person’s only way of communication would be through talking and hearing, while the deaf person can only type and see. With our device we bridge this gap and make it feasible. Our device makes it possible to make calls, send text messages, allowing them to check the date, time and temperature without the need of a mediator. It is also facilitated with GPS technology. This device caters to the basic needs of communication between the two impaired. This device is compact and portable and hence, this device allows a blind and a deaf to effectively communicate with each other on their own.
